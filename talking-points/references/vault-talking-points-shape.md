# Vault Talking Points Shape

The real Ship Sh!t Show talking points in the vault are not generic outlines. They usually look like this:

## Top block

- title
- stream or format note
- date and runtime
- one or more key source links near the top

## Main body

- `INTRO`
- `SECTION 1`
- `SECTION 2`
- `SECTION 3`
- `SECTION 4`
- `CONCLUSION`

Each section usually contains:

- a clear stance
- example bullets
- what to show or demo
- a strong quoted line or framing sentence

## Lower sections often included

- `Sources`
- `Key Stats`
- `Clips`
- `Distribution`

## House-style traits

- strong thesis early
- conflict and stakes are explicit
- fair acknowledgment before the harder take
- examples are concrete
- creator-friendly clip callouts are built into the notes
