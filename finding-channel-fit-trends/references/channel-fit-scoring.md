# Channel-Fit Scoring

Score channel fit on a 1-10 scale.

## Proven topic clusters from the vault

### 1. Model comparisons

Examples:

- GPT vs Claude
- benchmark verdicts
- "best model right now" narratives

### 2. Agent tooling

Examples:

- OpenClaw
- Claude Code
- Channels
- MCP workflows
- orchestration systems

### 3. Security, leaks, dangerous capability

Examples:

- breaches
- restricted releases
- "too powerful"
- chain-of-vulnerability stories

### 4. Product conflict

Examples:

- `X killed Y`
- one tool replacing another
- incumbents copying open source ideas

### 5. Existential change framing

Examples:

- job loss
- industry shift
- "AI changed everything"
- future-of-development inflection points

## Score weights

Add points for:

- direct overlap with proven topic clusters
- named tools or models already present in the vault
- obvious conflict framing
- strong security or capability stakes
- easy conversion into a thumbnail with one dominant center object
- clean split into livestream, long-form, or short

Subtract points for:

- hype with no clear angle for the channel
- abstract theory with no event, actor, or conflict
- stories that are huge but visually muddy
- generic startup news with no agent, tooling, benchmark, or security hook

## Urgency scoring

Urgency is also 1-10, but it is separate.

Raise urgency when:

- the story is breaking now
- creators are piling in quickly
- there is obvious audience confusion or controversy
- a fast response would matter

## Ranking rule

When two items compete:

- higher channel-fit wins
- urgency breaks the tie
- raw momentum is third
