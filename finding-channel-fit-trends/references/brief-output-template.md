# Brief Output Template

Use this exact shape for each ranked trend:

```text
1. <trend title>

Trend:
<one-line label>

Source Links:
- <link 1>
- <link 2>
- <link 3>

Why It Is Moving:
<short paragraph>

Why It Fits the Channel:
<short paragraph>

Recommended Angle:
<short paragraph>

Recommended Format:
<livestream | long-form video | short>

Title Hooks:
- <hook 1>
- <hook 2>
- <hook 3>

Thumbnail Idea Seed:
<one concrete visual seed>

Urgency Score:
<1-10>

Channel-Fit Score:
<1-10>
```

## Formatting rules

- Rank the briefs from strongest to weakest.
- Keep the link list flat.
- Keep the angle specific.
- Make the thumbnail seed concrete enough to hand directly to a thumbnail-prompt skill.
